Study Timetable Generator

Enter your subjects, their priorities, and any other commitments you have. The tool will generate a personalised weekly study timetable that you can download.

Step 1: Add Your Subjects

Add each subject you are studying and set its priority. Priority 1 is the lowest priority, priority 5 is the highest.

    Step 2: Add Your Commitments

    Add any fixed commitments that take up time in your week. This could be work, sport training, appointments, or anything else that happens at a set time.

      Step 3: Set Your Study Hours Per Day

      Choose how many hours you want to study each day. The timetable will fit as many study blocks as possible within these limits, working around your commitments.
